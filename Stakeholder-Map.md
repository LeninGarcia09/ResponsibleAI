## Stakeholder Roadshow Plan (Weeks 2–3)

**Purpose**: Brief key stakeholder groups on program mission, roadmap, and engagement model; gather input and secure buy-in.

**Format**: 45-minute sessions (30-minute presentation + 15-minute Q&A)

**Agenda**:
1. Business problem and "why now" (5 minutes)
2. Program mission and scope (5 minutes)
3. Roadmap and milestones (10 minutes)
4. Governance and quality bar (5 minutes)
5. Engagement model and how to participate (5 minutes)
6. Q&A and feedback (15 minutes)

**Sessions**:
- **Session 1**: Chief RAI Officer + RAI Leadership Team (Week 2, Mon)
- **Session 2**: Engineering Leadership + Solution Architects (Week 2, Tue)
- **Session 3**: Legal, Compliance, Privacy Teams (Week 2, Wed)
- **Session 4**: Security Architecture Team (Week 2, Thu)
- **Session 5**: Field Delivery + Customer Success Leadership (Week 2, Fri)
- **Session 6**: Partner Ecosystem Team (Week 3, Mon)
